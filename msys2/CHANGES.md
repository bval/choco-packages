* First Chocolatey MSYS2 release!
* Downloads 32bit or 64bit MSYS2 20150512.
* Contains **msystem.bat** 
  and convenience **msys2.bat**, **mingw32abat**, and **mingw64.bat**
  for activating **MSYS**, **MINGW32**, and **MINGW64** environments
